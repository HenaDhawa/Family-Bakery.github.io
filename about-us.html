FAMILY BAKERY JOURNEY



Baking Fresh, Healthy, Wholesome Products

Family Bakery is a food brand with the mission to provide high-quality bakery food at an affordable price to its customers. The brand believes that everyone deserves to indulge in delicious cakes and rusks at home or on the go. Its product portfolio is growing continuously, keeping in mind the market demand as well as the evolving food preferences of the population. Family Bakery is proud to have earned the love and trust of its loyal customer base in a very short time period.


Established in 1916, Family Bakery is part of the Purbasha Group and has been steadily following its vision of providing a fresh, healthy and wholesome experience to all its consumers. The Purbasha Group has a proven track-record of over 40 years of manufacturing excellence in foods products. It specialises in the contract packing of bakery foods items such as biscuits, breads, rusks and cake. Our commitment to quality and cost-control has earned the trust of the prominent national and international brands operating in the FMCG foods space, and has made us their preferred manufacturing partners in New York, East and West India.
